> The black box replacing these two will be taller than that Canary Island Date Palm

And its wonderful neighbor reduced to this. Because _density über alles_.

And of this one up at the corner—

![Magnolia Update — photograph 4](~/assets/images/blog/magnolia-update-04.webp)

I will say this, though, in defense of their replacements. They will absolutely define our age. In a generation, people will look at their [SimCity/Minecraft](https://www.dmagazine.com/frontburner/2018/12/were-still-trying-to-understand-why-new-apartments-are-so-ugly/) architectonic form and say yep, that’s _2020 in a nutshell_.

And then tear them the hell down.
