There's a cute little 1912 bungalow down at 6315 South Brynhurst Avenue, in the Hyde Park tract of Park Mesa Heights—

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-02.webp)

This is _exactly_ the kind of house a YIMBY loves to hate. The classic California bungalow—with front yard, a porch for sitting in the evenings, a backyard for an orange tree and your barbeque—absolutely emblematic of our shared collective memory and indispensable to our Angelenic nature, _and_ it has heritage trees!

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED — photograph 2](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-03.webp)

So we're taking in the tranquil atmosphere of our little bungalow friend when all of a sudden—

**\*KILL IT KILL IT KILL IT!!!!\*\*\*** \*\*ejaculate a herd of frenzied, frothing, jackbooted YIMBYs, jerking and jiggling while dark lord Scott Wiener pulls their strings.

**_BWA HA HA HA HA!_** guffaw a gaggle of drooling developers, extending bony index fingers at...the bungalow's _neighbor_! GASP! There's **_ALSO_** a **1928 MISSION REVIVAL BUNGALOW COURT \*\*\***RIGHT NEXT DOOR\*_\*\*!_

**_KILL IT KILL IT KILL IT!!!!_** they scream and scream and scream and scream until the wailing descends into an orgy of destruction as the assembled begin defiling all things beautiful, twirling and writhing in a debauch of bloodlust and madness!

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED — photograph 3](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-04.webp)

**_DEATH!_** they all scream in unison! **_DEATH! DEATH! DEATH!_** they chant, kicking their hooves together in a cackling cacophony of unholy terror!

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED — photograph 4](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-05.webp)

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED — photograph 5](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-06.webp)

All at once the little bungalow and courtyard housing explode into a great orange fireball, rising impossibly high into the heavens so as to singe the Heavenly Father, while the beasts writhe and gyrate atop a mountain of flaming white oak flooring!

And then they built this.

![The Bungalows of Hyde Park MUST BE SACRIFICED — photograph 6](~/assets/images/blog/the-bungalows-of-hyde-park-must-be-sacrificed-07.webp)

Whereupon God, bloodied but unbowed, singed but unsullied, gazed upon creation with heavy heart.
